Crafting Elegant Digital Solutions

I started my coding journey building simple static web pages. Over the years, that curiosity developed into a deep passion for building high-performing interactive web apps. I love solving complex structural challenges and making sure user interfaces feel fluid, clean, and intuitive.

Career Goals

My goal is to work on scale-demanding web systems, contribute actively to open-source UI libraries, and master modern cloud architecture. I aim to create technology that leaves a positive, lasting impact on its users.

In addition to web programming, I devote significant time to studying design systems, color theory, and micro-interactions. I believe a good engineer understands design constraints, and a good designer understands engineering feasibility. This dual perspective allows me to build robust systems without sacrificing visual fidelity.
